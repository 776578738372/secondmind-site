How it works

Just say it

No forms, no categories to pick. Write a reminder the way you'd tell a person — SecondMind understands the rest.

It decides the urgency

A missed medicine reminder gets followed up. A missed birthday note doesn't get repeated five times. SecondMind tells the difference.

It knows when to wait

Busy in a meeting? Non-urgent nudges hold off until you're free — nothing important ever gets buried, and nothing trivial interrupts you.
