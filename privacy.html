Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

SecondMind ("we," "us," "our") is a personal reminder assistant available on WhatsApp and the web. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Your phone number and name, used to identify your account and deliver reminders via WhatsApp.
  • The text of reminders you create (e.g. "call mom tomorrow at 3pm"), which we process to extract the task, timing, and category.
  • Basic usage data, such as when reminders are created, fired, snoozed, or completed, so the app can track your task history and improve reminder timing.

How we use it

We use this information solely to operate SecondMind: understanding what you want to be reminded about, scheduling and delivering that reminder at the right time, and letting you manage your tasks. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

Third parties involved

Meta / WhatsApp Business Platform — used to send and receive your messages. Meta's own privacy practices govern how they handle message delivery infrastructure.

Groq (LLM provider) — the text of your reminders is sent to Groq's API to interpret natural language into structured task data (what, when, how important). Groq processes this text but does not use it to train models on our behalf.

Data storage

Your tasks and account details are stored securely in our database. We retain this data for as long as your account is active, so your reminder history and preferences remain available to you.

Your choices

  • You can delete individual tasks at any time from within the app.
  • You can request full account deletion by contacting us at the email below — we will remove your stored data upon request.
  • You can stop using SecondMind on WhatsApp at any time by simply no longer messaging the number; this does not automatically delete stored data, which requires a separate deletion request.

Children's privacy

SecondMind is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of SecondMind after changes means you accept the updated policy.
